A structured checklist designed to help teams audit their Amplitude implementation and ensure reliable, accurate analytics that drive confident decisions.

## What's Inside

### Event Audit
Review event names, consistency, and volume to identify gaps and redundancies in your tracking.

### User Properties Review
Assess how user properties are tracked and whether they support the segmentation you need.

### Data Quality and Governance
Check for duplicates, missing data, and errors that could be undermining your analysis.

### Action Plan
Document issues, fixes, and priorities with a clear path to improvement.

## Who It's For

- Product managers responsible for analytics quality
- Data teams maintaining Amplitude implementations
- Growth teams who need to trust their data
- Startups looking to build a scalable analytics foundation
