A hands-on playbook for building in-app experiences that drive feature adoption, reduce support costs, and deliver personalized content to your users.

## What's Inside

### Resource Centers
Step-by-step guide to setting up and managing in-app resource centers that help users find what they need.

### Guides and Walkthroughs
Breakdown of guide templates including Tours, Tooltips, Announcements, Banners, and Checklists, with best practices for each.

### Surveys
Survey design strategies to maximize response rates, with setup checklists for publishing.

### User Targeting
Best practices for cohort-based user targeting by behavior, plan type, or lifecycle stage to deliver the right experience to the right user.

### Implementation Tips
Hierarchy workarounds, throttle settings, and a quick-reference feature comparison table.

## Who It's For

- Product Managers building in-app experiences
- Customer Success teams looking to improve onboarding
- Growth and Marketing leaders driving feature adoption
- Amplitude users seeking structured engagement strategies
