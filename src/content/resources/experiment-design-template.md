Stop wasting time on inconclusive tests. This template provides a systematic approach to experiment planning and execution that delivers reliable, actionable results.

## What's Inside

### Hypothesis Definition
A step-by-step framework for clearly defining what you're testing and the expected impact.

### Success Metrics
Guided process for establishing primary and secondary metrics that actually measure what matters.

### Sample Size Calculation
Methodology to determine the right sample size and prevent false positives from ruining your results.

### Tracking Setup Verification
Checklists to verify your tracking is correctly configured before launching an experiment.

### Results Analysis
Guidance for extracting meaningful insights from your test data and making confident decisions.

## Who It's For

- Growth and experimentation teams
- Product managers and analysts
- Conversion optimization marketers
- Anyone who wants to make smarter decisions through properly structured testing
