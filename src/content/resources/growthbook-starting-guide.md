This playbook covers everything you need to get started with GrowthBook and scale your experimentation program.

## What's Inside

### Setting Up GrowthBook
Configuration of feature flags, SDKs, and integrations to get your experimentation infrastructure ready.

### Integration Guidance
Step-by-step instructions for connecting GrowthBook with Amplitude, GA4, BigQuery, Snowflake, and other data warehouses.

### Experiment Implementation and Analysis
A structured approach to designing, running, and interpreting A/B tests with confidence.

### Metrics, Feature Flags, and Traffic Management
Custom metric setup, feature flag best practices, and traffic allocation strategies to ensure statistically valid results.

### Scaling Best Practices
Monitoring, optimization, and expansion strategies for taking successful tests to full rollout.

## Who It's For

- Growth and product teams looking to establish or scale experimentation
- Engineers and analysts setting up GrowthBook infrastructure
- Marketers and decision-makers who want to validate ideas with data
