A custom ChatGPT that generates standardized campaign naming conventions and documentation, ensuring your marketing team maintains consistent naming across all platforms and campaigns.

## How to Use It

1. Open the custom GPT link in ChatGPT
2. Describe your marketing channels, platforms, and campaign types
3. The GPT generates a tailored naming convention framework
4. Use the output to standardize naming across your team

## What You'll Get

- Consistent campaign naming conventions across all channels
- Documentation template for onboarding new team members
- Standardized taxonomy that improves attribution accuracy
- Cleaner analytics data from properly named campaigns
