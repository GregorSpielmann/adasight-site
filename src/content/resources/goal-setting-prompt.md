A ready-to-use AI prompt template that helps teams structure their goal-setting process and create actionable growth plans.

## How to Use It

1. Open the Google Doc template
2. Copy the prompt structure
3. Use it with your preferred AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
4. Customize the inputs to match your business context
5. Generate structured, actionable goal documentation

## What You'll Get

- A structured framework for defining growth objectives
- Clear alignment between team goals and business outcomes
- Actionable plans with measurable milestones
- Documentation template for sharing goals across teams
