A ready-to-use growth model spreadsheet designed for e-commerce businesses that want to forecast revenue and simulate the compounding effects of performance improvements.

## What's Inside

### Marketing Input Fields
Configure your ad spend, growth rate, and Marketing Efficiency Ratio to model different scenarios.

### Revenue Forecasting
Calculations based on conversions and clicks that project monthly revenue under various conditions.

### Cohort Projections
Month-by-month income growth projections showing how improvements compound over time.

### Pre-Built Dashboard
Forecasting logic visualization that makes it easy to present scenarios to stakeholders.

## Who It's For

- Direct-to-consumer brands and marketplace operators
- Performance marketers managing ad budgets
- Founders planning growth strategy
- RevOps teams modeling revenue scenarios

## Key Features

- Predictive modeling under different growth scenarios
- Decision-making support for budget allocation and conversion strategy
- Pre-filled formulas enabling immediate use without historical data requirements
