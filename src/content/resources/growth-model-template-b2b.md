A ready-to-use B2B growth forecasting tool that enables you to model revenue across the full customer lifecycle from acquisition through expansion and churn.

## What's Inside

### Funnel Stage Tracking
Model performance across Acquisition, Onboarding, Retention, Expansion, and Churn stages.

### Revenue Segmentation
Break down revenue by cohort based on retention milestones to understand the true value of each customer segment.

### Impact Simulation
Test different onboarding and churn scenarios to visualize their effect on future revenue.

### Pre-Built Formulas
Clean structure with pre-filled formulas for immediate use without requiring a data team.

## Who It's For

- B2B SaaS founders and leadership teams
- Growth and marketing teams managing acquisition and churn metrics
- Organizations requiring data-backed financial projections
- Revenue operations teams planning capacity and spend
