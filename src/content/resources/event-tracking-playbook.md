Want to understand how users interact with your product and make better business decisions? This playbook breaks down event tracking into clear, actionable steps with industry-specific examples.

## What's Inside

### Foundational Principles
Basic concepts of event tracking and how to measure user interactions effectively.

### Industry-Specific Examples
Real-world tracking plan implementations across five sectors: eCommerce, SaaS, Gaming, Healthcare, and Finance.

### Implementation Systems
Step-by-step processes for setting up data collection and analysis from scratch.

### Best Practices
Strategies for maintaining tracking consistency and accuracy as your product scales.

## What You'll Learn

- Event tracking fundamentals and why they matter for business decisions
- Real-life implementation examples you can adapt to your context
- How to create a tracking plan aligned with your business objectives
- Data consistency practices for scalable, reliable event tracking

## Who It's For

- Marketers looking to understand user behavior
- Product managers setting up tracking for the first time
- Startup founders who need to get analytics right from the start
