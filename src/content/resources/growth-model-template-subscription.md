A subscription-focused growth model with pre-filled formulas for acquisition funnels, billing projections, churn modeling, and lifetime value tracking.

## What's Inside

### Acquisition Funnel Tracking
Model the full path from sign-up to paying subscriber with conversion rates at each stage.

### Monthly Recurring Revenue Estimates
Project MRR based on your acquisition rate, pricing, and churn assumptions.

### Retention vs. Churn Logic
Built-in formulas that forecast income based on different retention and churn scenarios.

### Lifetime Value Simulation
Test different scenarios to understand how changes in retention affect customer lifetime value.

## Who It's For

- B2C SaaS and digital subscription founders
- Growth and marketing teams managing acquisition and churn
- Anyone seeking a clear financial model for a subscription business

## Key Features

- Predictive modeling for subscriber growth scenarios
- Data-informed decision support for spend and retention strategy
- Pre-filled formulas requiring minimal setup for immediate use
