Stop creating messy or unreliable campaign URLs that skew your analytics data. This template standardizes UTM tagging across your entire marketing team.

## What's Inside

### UTM Tagging Builder
Generates properly formatted campaign URLs using dropdown inputs so you never have to manually construct UTM strings.

### Medium and Source Reference Tab
Pre-filled, customizable lists of common medium and source values to ensure consistent tagging across campaigns.

### Reference Tab
Clear explanations of proper usage for each UTM parameter (source, medium, campaign) so your team follows the same conventions.

## Who It's For

- Marketing teams requiring consistent tracking across campaigns
- Growth marketers managing multiple channels simultaneously
- Teams struggling with unreliable UTM implementation and inaccurate attribution data
