A customizable spreadsheet with guided workflows that helps you audit your GA4 setup and plan improvements without requiring deep technical expertise.

## What's Inside

### Onboarding Questionnaire
Clarify your analytics objectives and define what to track before you start making changes.

### GA4 Audit Checklist
Assess your current GA4 implementation to identify gaps and configuration issues.

### Project Management Tab
Track audit tasks, deadlines, and progress in one place.

### Customizable Tracking Plan
Document events, parameters, and conversions in a structured framework.

## Who It's For

- Growth, marketing, and product teams
- Implementation agencies managing client setups
- Founders and operations leads seeking reliable data infrastructure
- Non-technical users who need to audit analytics without deep coding knowledge

## Key Features

- Compatible with Google Tag Manager
- Works with existing tracking setups
- Designed for non-technical users
