## Best Practices for Maximizing Value from Amplitude AI Agents as a Product Manager

[Effective use of segmentation keywords](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2497941?hl=en) is essential for unlocking actionable insights with Amplitude AI Agents. The way you structure your queries directly impacts the relevance and depth of the results:

### 1. Be specific with segmentation keywords

Instead of generic terms, use clear identifiers such as `user cohort`, `subscription plan`, or `geographic region`. For example, asking *“What features do power users in the EU region engage with most after onboarding?”* provides richer context than a broad query like *“Which features are most popular?”*

### 2. Layer multiple segments

Combine attributes (e.g., device type + acquisition channel) to surface nuanced patterns. *“Show churn rates by device type among users acquired via paid search”* uncovers insights that drive targeted strategies.

### 3. Iterate and refine

Start broad, then narrow down based on initial results. If an answer looks too generic, add or adjust segmentation keywords to drill deeper.

*Double-checking query context* remains critical when working with AI-driven analytics. Automated insights deliver speed and convenience, but product managers should always validate findings before making decisions:

> “AI Agents process large volumes of data quickly, but no model is immune to misinterpretation if the query is vague or contextually off-target.”

### 4. Review response assumptions

Look for metadata or footnotes AI Agents provide about their analysis scope and filters applied.

### 5. Cross-check with raw data

When possible, verify key takeaways against underlying reports or dashboards, especially for high-stakes decisions.

### 6. Leverage team knowledge

Discuss ambiguous findings with stakeholders who understand user behavior nuances; human judgment often fills in gaps where AI may oversimplify.

By mastering both segmentation keyword usage and diligent review of automated outputs, product managers place themselves in the best position to extract real value from Amplitude AI Agents. This approach ensures insights are not only fast but also trustworthy and relevant to evolving product goals.
