### What is a Martech stack?

A **Martech stack** is the set of software tools used by marketing and growth teams to attract, convert, and retain customers. It includes tools for email, automation, analytics, attribution, CRM, and more.

### What is a Martech stack audit?

A **Martech stack audit** is the process of reviewing and analyzing your current tools to remove redundancies, improve integration, and ensure alignment with business goals.
