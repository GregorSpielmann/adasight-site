At Adasight, we’re committed to helping growth teams turn data into scalable revenue streams. To do this well, we surround ourselves with the very best minds in analytics and experimentation.

That’s why we’re excited to announce that **Dr. Simon Jackson** has joined Adasight in an **advisory role**.

## About Simon

Simon brings a track record of leading experimentation and growth at **Meta, Canva, and Booking.com**. Across these companies, he built and scaled experimentation programs that influenced product direction, marketing impact, and customer experience for millions of users worldwide.

Today, Simon works as an **independent consultant** and thought leader in the APAC region. He recently launched **The Experimenter’s Calculator**, a free tool designed to help startups and scaleups run smarter experiments without needing a statistics background.

## Why This Partnership Matters

This is a **partnership, not a full-time role**—and that’s exactly what makes it valuable. Simon will act as a **trusted advisor**, sharing his expertise and providing strategic input as we expand our experimentation and analytics offerings.

Through this collaboration, we aim to:

- **Bring best practices from global tech leaders** into the hands of startups and scaleups.
- **Strengthen our experimentation frameworks** with insights from someone who’s run programs at the highest level.
- **Co-shape our product and service direction**, ensuring experimentation remains simple, practical, and impactful for growth teams.

## Looking Ahead

We’re excited to work with Simon in this advisory capacity and see this as the beginning of a long-term partnership. His perspective will help us deliver even greater value to our clients—and push the boundaries of what’s possible in growth analytics.
