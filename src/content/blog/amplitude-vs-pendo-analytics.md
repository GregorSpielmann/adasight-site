Product managers and marketers use [product analytics tools](https://www.adasight.com/partner/amplitude) to understand how users interact with their products. These tools typically use event tracking, user segmentation, and data analysis to identify user behavior, such as what steps users take before purchasing a product or what marketing tactics turn website visitors into customers. By identifying such properties, product managers can decide what marketing channels to invest in and how to make the user journey easier.

## What is product analytics?

Product analytics is the process of analyzing data on how users interact with a product or service. This data reveals customer engagement with a product rather than self-reported behavior. As a result, analytics product managers can improve their offerings and invest in high ROI (Return on Investment) marketing efforts.

Multiple product analytics tools are available, each with a unique set of characteristics. [Amplitude](https://amplitude.com/) and [Pendo product analytics](https://www.pendo.io/) are among the tools product managers prefer. However, both have different attributes that make them suitable for different use cases. For example, Amplitude product analytics is known for real-time reporting, user segmentation, and custom event tracking among users. On the other hand, Pendo product analytics is known for its easy interface and retrospective reporting.

### Data modeling and user identification

![Amplitude unique user tracking](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6539206f401d118f32c193e5/66b71af8017c8bf6ba0ff196_AD_4nXc3E88CdUM0312ckAS2Cmwc0aOjeQMPDpUDQdSPAVi1cyRhbIuYQA2Ze1g2dird-EG2XH_AorzBTJ4RljshjcMQgu-8tDKSqYfyk1sNnWFMuBZfo7TqjIMfRpHbqcX5NzaTqt7INxNJPAfqz1ACjKpgk3Ho.png)

*Source*

Amplitude offers real-time data modeling to analyze features that drive growth to your business. These features include retention, funnels, revenue, cohort, and flexible user segmentation analysis. These capabilities reveal where and how customers convert, compare metrics, and identify growth driving cross-platform behavior.

Amplitude uses three methods to identify users:

- Device IDs
- Amplitude IDs
- User IDs

It automatically generates an Amplitude ID for each user and allows product analysts (or anyone using Amplitude for product analysis) to create user IDs. For users who interact with your platform without logging in or creating an account, Amplitude assigns an anonymous ID. This ID tracks all activities under that session, and once the user is identified, the system merges the anonymous data with the user’s profile. This mechanism helps measure the effectiveness of your efforts to convert visitors into customers and identify any obstacles in the conversion process.

Pendo analytics offers in-depth product analysis using numerous features, including user paths, funnels, retention analysis, and feedback modules. Pendo uses user IDs, user attributes, and session tracking to identify users. It also tracks anonymous users using anonymous IDs.

## How to find the best analytics tools for product managers?

Amplitude and Pendo are considered the best product analytics software by their users for multiple reasons. However, since each company has unique product data analytics needs based on its business model, growth, and customer base, finding a suitable solution for your use case requires careful analysis of business needs.

Finding the best analytics tools depends on various factors, including the analytics tool's features and your specific product management analytics needs. Other factors to consider include [customization options](https://www.adasight.com/blog/10-amplitude-configuration-mistakes-you-should-avoid), event tracking, data diversity, scalability, integration options, data compliance, reporting, and ease of use.

In contrast, Pendo is suitable product analytics software for businesses primarily looking for easy setup in exchange for data lag and comprehensive user behavior analysis.

## FAQs:

### What is the difference between Amplitude and Pendo?

Amplitude offers advanced and customizable analytics features along with flexible data tracking and extensive integration portfolio. This makes it must have product analytics tools for businesses with growing data, advanced analytics, and growth identification needs.

Pendo offers simplified event tracking and quick onboarding with limited features in the free plan.

### What makes Amplitude a scalable product analytics tool?

Amplitude scales in three dimensions, i.e., data volume, price, and number of users, without compromising performance. Its pre-aggregation and lambda architecture help reduce resources and maintain real-time updates.

### How do Amplitude and Pendo handle data?

Amplitude follows industry standards and complies with relevant data protection regulations such as GDPR and CCPA. It provides users control over events and properties definitions, making it a strong choice among analytics tools for business.

Pendo also prioritizes data security and protection by adhering to regulatory schemes, including GDPR and HIPAA.
