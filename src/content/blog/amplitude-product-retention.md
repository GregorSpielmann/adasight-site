## Understanding product retention

Product retention is the ability of a company to keep its customers engaged with its products and encourage them to return for future purchases, sessions, or interactions. It is crucial for the business model, as failing to retain customers can significantly impact the bottom line.

Here’s why product retention matters for your business:

- **Cost efficiency:** Acquiring new customers is often more expensive than retaining existing ones. High retention rates reduce marketing and acquisition costs, making the business more cost-efficient.

- **Revenue stability:** Retained customers provide a consistent revenue stream, contributing to financial stability and growth. They are also more likely to repeat purchases or subscribe to additional services.

- **Competitive advantage:** High retention rates can set a company apart. When customers consistently choose to stay with a product, it signals to the market that it is reliable and superior in meeting user needs.
