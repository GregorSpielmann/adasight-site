## The Challenge

The online training brand struggled with funnel optimization and lacked time for data analysis. Key issues included high user drop-offs on landing pages, inability to identify bottlenecks in customer journeys, poor landing page design decisions driven by guesswork, and difficulty determining which marketing initiatives generated quality traffic for cross-channel optimization.

## What We Did

Adasight implemented a comprehensive data-driven funnel analysis:

1. **Campaign funnel evaluation** -- Assessed end-to-end performance across marketing campaigns
2. **User experience bottleneck identification** -- Used Google Analytics to pinpoint where users dropped off and why
3. **Campaign performance review** -- Identified top and underperforming campaigns with specific improvement recommendations
4. **Landing page heat map implementation** -- Deployed heat mapping to understand user interaction patterns
5. **Actionable optimization recommendations** -- Provided data-backed recommendations based on key event data including clicks to register, webinar registrations, and checkouts

## The Results

The engagement delivered enhanced user engagement metrics with improved page views and time on site, higher conversion rates from lead to customer, decreased bounce rates with increased user retention, improved marketing ROI and channel effectiveness, growth in new customer acquisition with lower acquisition costs, and increased landing page conversion rates.
