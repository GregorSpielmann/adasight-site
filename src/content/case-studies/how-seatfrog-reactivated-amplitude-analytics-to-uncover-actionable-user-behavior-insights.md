## The Challenge

Seatfrog, a travel-tech platform that lets passengers bid on premium seat upgrades, had invested in Amplitude but wasn't actively using it for insights. Their analytics infrastructure was dormant and not driving decision-making. With separate iOS and Android projects creating cross-platform complexity, the team had no understanding of user engagement patterns or lifecycle dynamics, lacked structured behavioral segmentation, and had multiple critical business questions that remained unanswered.

## What We Did

Adasight executed a focused two-sprint engagement with Seatfrog's product team.

**Sprint 1** focused on deep product discovery mapping, exploratory data analysis, dashboard development with documented insights, platform comparison analysis between iOS and Android, and collaborative team sessions to align on priorities.

**Sprint 2** delivered a targeted business question framework, decision-oriented dashboards addressing specific business needs, behavioral segmentation analysis, comprehensive documentation, and knowledge transfer to enable the team to conduct independent analysis going forward.

## The Results

Within four weeks, Seatfrog had reactivated their dormant Amplitude implementation and transformed it into an active decision-making tool. The team gained behavioral clarity across platforms, self-service analytics capability, a reusable and expandable framework, and faster decision-making confidence backed by documented data insights and ongoing monitoring capabilities.
