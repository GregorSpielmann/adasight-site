## The Challenge

BetDEX, a betting and gaming platform, required a dependable analytics infrastructure to comprehend user actions and measure conversion performance across their system. Key obstacles included securing team alignment on uniform event naming conventions, ensuring collected data answered critical business questions, and establishing more accurate tracking during user registration to enhance conversion measurement.

## What We Did

Adasight partnered with BetDEX to strengthen their analytics through Amplitude:

1. **Business question definition** -- Identified critical business inquiries and established key use cases for data gathering
2. **User behavior taxonomy** -- Constructed a clear framework for analyzing and categorizing user actions
3. **Registration flow monitoring** -- Emphasized tracking via button clicks and event triggers throughout the registration process
4. **Cross-department standardization** -- Collaborated across teams to implement standardized event naming practices

## The Results

The engagement delivered improved data tracking and analytics capabilities across the platform, with multiple use cases defined addressing key business questions. A clear user behavior taxonomy enabled systematic analysis, conversion tracking was optimized through precise registration process monitoring, and standardized event naming conventions were adopted across all teams. This gave BetDEX enhanced conversion rate visibility, improved user onboarding optimization, and better platform understanding supporting confident decision-making.
