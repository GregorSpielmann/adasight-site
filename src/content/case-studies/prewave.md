## The Challenge

Prewave, an AI-powered supply chain risk management platform, needed to strengthen analytics capabilities for their growing product. Key challenges included unclear success milestones, underutilized datasets and technology, platform usability limitations, and the need for a cohesive analytics framework supporting decision-making across the organization.

## What We Did

Adasight implemented a multi-phase approach:

1. **Problem scoping** -- aligned solutions with business objectives and identified the highest-impact areas
2. **Use case and KPI definition** -- collaborated with the team to define frameworks measuring what matters
3. **Tech and data evaluation** -- assessed existing datasets and technology to identify untapped opportunities
4. **UX/UI feedback** -- provided targeted recommendations to improve platform usability
5. **Tracking plan implementation** -- built comprehensive tracking to capture key user behaviors
6. **Dashboard and report creation** -- delivered custom dashboards enabling real-time progress tracking

## The Results

The engagement delivered enhanced platform usability through targeted UX/UI feedback, a comprehensive analytics framework supporting better decision-making, dashboards and reports enabling ongoing progress tracking, and identification of growth opportunities within existing datasets and technology.
