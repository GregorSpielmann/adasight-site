## The Challenge

Wickey, an e-commerce company, operated with a partial tracking configuration where GA4 events fed into Amplitude, undermining data consistency and user behavior insights. Disconnected data sources produced duplicate or inaccurate events. The organization lacked clearly defined funnels or KPIs measuring e-commerce performance across discovery-to-purchase stages, and experimentation structure within Amplitude was absent, preventing confident A/B testing.

## What We Did

Adasight delivered a comprehensive implementation:

1. **KPI definition and funnel mapping** aligned with business goals across the full e-commerce journey
2. **Tailored Amplitude tracking plan** with detailed event specifications and properties
3. **Implementation guidance** to the development team ensuring proper event tracking and business logic alignment
4. **Thorough validation** confirming Amplitude events flowed correctly while GA4 data was decoupled
5. **Initial dashboards** for KPI monitoring and actionable insight identification
6. **Experimentation enablement session** equipping teams with Amplitude Experiment knowledge

## The Results

The engagement delivered unified, reliable data through native Amplitude tracking ensuring consistency and accuracy. Clear funnel visibility emerged across key conversion points. Marketing and product teams gained experimentation capability to run controlled A/B tests. Dashboards surfaced KPIs supporting cross-team decision-making, enabling faster, data-driven choices.
