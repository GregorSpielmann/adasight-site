## The Challenge

Tonic, a music education app, faced a critical growth phase requiring new insights to guide their next steps. Data was not being effectively utilized for decision-making, the team lacked clear Key Performance Indicators, identifying growth drivers and bottlenecks was difficult, and they needed better understanding of user behavior to improve engagement and retention.

## What We Did

Adasight implemented a multi-faceted approach:

1. **Data integrity audit** -- Reviewed the full stack across Segment, Amplitude, and Braze to ensure data accuracy and consistency
2. **Growth metrics refinement** -- Defined and refined key metrics around sign-up, retention, and stickiness
3. **User behavior analysis** -- Analyzed behavior across the full engagement spectrum to identify patterns
4. **Duolingo Growth States Model** -- Implemented this proven framework for user segmentation, enabling Tonic to understand where users sit in their lifecycle
5. **Leadership alignment** -- Facilitated collaborative strategy sessions to ensure everyone was working from the same data
6. **Tech stack audit and optimization** -- Assessed and optimized the existing technology infrastructure
7. **Activation and retention analysis** -- Conducted rigorous analysis of user activation triggers and retention drivers

## The Results

The engagement delivered better user engagement, stronger retention outcomes, and a solid growth path in the music education sector. The implementation of the Duolingo Growth States Model provided a powerful framework for ongoing user segmentation and lifecycle management.
