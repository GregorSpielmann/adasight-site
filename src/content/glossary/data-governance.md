## What is Data Governance?

Data governance is the set of policies, processes, and standards that ensure data is accurate, consistent, secure, and used responsibly within an organization. It involves managing the availability, usability, integrity, and security of data used across business operations.

## An Example to Understand Data Governance

A company might establish data governance policies that define who can access certain types of customer data, how data should be cleaned and validated, and how long data should be stored to ensure it remains accurate and secure.

## Benefits of Using Data Governance

- **Ensures Data Quality**: Proper governance ensures that data is accurate, complete, and up-to-date.
- **Enhances Compliance**: Helps businesses comply with regulations such as GDPR and HIPAA by controlling access to sensitive data and ensuring it is handled properly.
- **Improves Decision-Making**: Reliable and consistent data is crucial for making informed decisions and driving business growth.

## Why is Data Governance Important for Startups and SaaS?

For startups and SaaS businesses, data governance ensures that customer, product, and business data is properly managed, which is essential for making data-driven decisions, protecting customer privacy, and maintaining regulatory compliance.
